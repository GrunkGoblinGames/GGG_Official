Beneath the Mantle

This game is still in development

Beneath the Mantle, is a game where you have been commisioned to pilot the C.O.R.E., a large drill through the moon Enceladus, with the goal of discovering what is at its core, and returning your findings to AVA so they can pass the infomation on to their client. However the journey may not go as planned and you will have to adapt and maintain control of the vessel if you hope to make it back alive.
